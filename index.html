Architecture & U.S. Compliance Standards

This application simulates and streams real-time prescription refill events using a Python serverless backend deployed on Vercel, paired with an Apple-inspired minimalist frontend designed for zero friction.

Authoritative U.S. Data Sources

  • FDA & Drugs@FDA: Real-time regulatory approvals, indications, and safety communications.
  • DailyMed (NIH / NLM): Official Structured Product Labeling (SPL) and package inserts.
  • AHFS & PDR: Clinical compendia for therapeutic guidelines, off-label uses, and drug interactions.
